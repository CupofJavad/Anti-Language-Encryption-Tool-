450 Le Eg yass yn ynduin cyeatlwg va ye edan Le ud rig olnin, id dadun cyuy cywik gard. Rin tdawelwg Eg weni, cyom cyuy iys som, Nenugt alrir engilt, in doenop til myeb tdaspsom. Fagvelfag ud dawe edwr, rit udun drodu, Alt naff til imyn rit ud teynys y; In ud dawe myebr ogdu rig naff feltsamnyff, Taldynen le Eg cyniti ud fenlys myebr cyom cyad.
