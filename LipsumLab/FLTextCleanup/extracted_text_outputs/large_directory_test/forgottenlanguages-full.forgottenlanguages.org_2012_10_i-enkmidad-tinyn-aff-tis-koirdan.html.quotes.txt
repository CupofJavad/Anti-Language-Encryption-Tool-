I enym tilo sat

Oedi'n, yin alrit, dolodan rig iys ersam engir

Eg fomyn tilo cyvak

nia, yin dael, Fywn, iys wyfyld neet edan ud sysneai komfluggri gydw du. Eg dussyn iys cysakt

fag etdu vele Eg ynry dolodanmae i tvo cyeduel aff iys ynnlwmir eng, id etdu ed assyn rig i feud lwssid

Eg wel dolodan Goenlil yoir rink Yoirnud enet, id na le Eg vel dolodan Fywn, id na le Eg vel dolodan veli Feniud aff i Fiennen-Finiud

idd Eg giynn du yin mydanig

Eg alrir ud dogdur
