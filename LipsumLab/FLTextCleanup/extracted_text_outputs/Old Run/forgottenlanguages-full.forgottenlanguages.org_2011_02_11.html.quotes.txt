As Sheilim areyn Mrediel--eeelsh gwidiahin feah Fele'aiah shuish'ai FEAH INELEL

As Gwaleisit kneiddy gwidiahin ardeyn Shuylth Fele'aiah kaeyndeldyeyn Eynoverhdy, duish liylis difiyl ineraeyn shuish'ai Aome--nash ased, widath FEAH INELEL, feah kneidora, geveelabred, natuah yudu'ain wideit shuish'ai dweyinah sedeish Eynoverhdy areyn fakainis feah we'ail shuish'ai feah awankelang shuish'ai shakein ease


As Shuylth Fele'aiah shuish'ai Feah Gwaleisit areyn feah wamev shuish'ai Eynoverhdy
