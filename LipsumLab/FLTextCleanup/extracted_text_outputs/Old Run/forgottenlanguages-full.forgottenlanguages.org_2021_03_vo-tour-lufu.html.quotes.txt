If love is desired for consolation on earth, then the person stays at the first step in the categories identified as the progression of spiritual evolution described in Islamic mysticism. The joy of love is something, but searching for love for its joy is something else. In the former, there is joy, a happiness as a result of love caused by knowing God. In the latter, there is a will to lean to love in the cessation of the search for joy

In Islamic mysticism, one thus does not have an anxiety about death but has a separation anxiety. Separation anxiety is not about death but an existential anxiety and is not shared at a conscious level by everybody; it is an anxiety experienced only by lovers
