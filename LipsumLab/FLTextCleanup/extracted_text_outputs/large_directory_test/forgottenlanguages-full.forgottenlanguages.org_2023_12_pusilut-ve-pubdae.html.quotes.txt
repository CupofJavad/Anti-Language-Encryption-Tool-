Any transmutation is an act of creation, and the reasons for that creation is always a revelation

Sacred, religious texts make claims for absolute truth, and supposedly a life lived according to their instructions would be considered as more truthful, from the point of view of the believers

The division between good and evil, the angelic and the demonic, is wrongly translated into a division between passive power and active will. The opposite, however, is the only truth: in an evil world, passivity is demonic, and active resistance is angelic
