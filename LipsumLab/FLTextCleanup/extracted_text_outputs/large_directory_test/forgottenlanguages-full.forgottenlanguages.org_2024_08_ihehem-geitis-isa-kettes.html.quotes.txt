So although we are framed a natural body, yet many find no predominance over nature, and cause such wonderful, sudden, and difficult operations, as shall Angels and spirits obey us, the stars are disordered, the Heavenly powers compelled, the Elements made obedient, and so on

After the Fall, Satanael created the world, with all its plants and animals. He formed Adam from mud, but failed to animate him. He appealed to god the Father who animated Adam with the divine spirit. This is why the body can be from one god but the spirit from another. As their bodies were evil by nature few men ever reached heaven, and those who did were immediatly slaughtered by angels past the Gates of Heaven
