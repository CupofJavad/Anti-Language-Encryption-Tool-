A civilization is a negotiated order, and as long as each member of that civilization participates with equal rights in that negotiation, the civilization will persist. Otherwise, civilization will fade into nothingness

Symbolization increases the efficiency of finding and quantifying information from systems, reduces sensitivity to measurement noise, and discriminates both specific and general classes of explanatory models. But that symbolization only makes sense to members of the system that wishes to be explained from within the system; to an outside observer, the symbolization is just cryptography
