He practiced the divinatory arts day and night, to the point of exhaustion. And he did it for years. But the result was always the same: nothing. All the cards, all the visions, always showed darkness. When on his deathbed, his master approached him and, bending down towards him, whispered in his ear: You always tried to see the future, and you really succeeded in seeing it, each and every time you tried. You saw nothingness

The beast that nests in you always invents demons; it spits them out, and with your imagination, endows them with life. Everything that frightens you is born of you, and everything that terrifies you has your own face. There is no one out there. Only you

Right now, at this very moment, your tomorrow becomes today. By the time you finish reading this sentence, your today will have become the dust of yesterday
